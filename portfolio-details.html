Formula 1 Racing Technology

Formula 1 is the highest class of international racing for single-seater formula racing cars. Teams compete in Grand Prix events worldwide using advanced hybrid engines and aerodynamic technology. Top drivers such as Max Verstappen, Lewis Hamilton, and Charles Leclerc race for the Formula 1 World Championship every season. F1 cars can reach speeds over 350 km/h and represent the pinnacle of motorsport engineering and innovation.
